# Calendar Split Timeline Study

## Goal

Refine the selected calendar-split timeline into one focused prototype. Trips remain the page's visual subject; school holidays provide quiet calendar context.

## Layout

- Remove the first three holiday treatments and their selector.
- Keep the timeline centered.
- Add a two-state month-rule control: label above the rule and label below the rule.
- Extend each thin month rule from the label's left edge to the timeline without crossing the axis.
- Apply the same treatment to the year break, with a heavier year rule.

## Hierarchy

- Use lighter-weight month labels.
- Increase holiday-band contrast enough to reveal duration at a glance, while keeping it below trip colors.
- Show only holiday dates. Omit holiday names and the holiday legend.
- Center long-trip details vertically against their trip markers. Keep short-trip details aligned to the marker start.

## Verification

- Compare both month-rule states in the browser.
- Confirm holiday dates remain legible for long and short periods.
- Confirm month and year rules stop before the timeline.
- Confirm Lake Garda and Japan details sit at each marker's vertical center.
